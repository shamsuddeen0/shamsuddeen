## üÜì Free Resources (Start Here)
*If you are just starting and have $0 budget, look at these first.*

### **[Cisco NetAcad: Ethical Hacker](https://www.netacad.com/courses/ethical-hacker?courseLang=en-US)**
*   **Cost:** Free
*   **Why I recommend it:** It covers the absolute fundamentals of offensive security in a structured way. Great for building a solid foundation before jumping into tools.

### **[Hacking Scenarios (Cloud Labs)](https://hackingscenarios.com)**
*   **Cost:** Free / Paid tiers
*   **Why I recommend it:** Browser-based labs. You don't need to install VMs or configure networks. Just click and hack.
